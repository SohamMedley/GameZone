How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control the ball
  • Avoid obstacles and collect points
  • Collect power-ups for special abilities:
    • Shield - Protects from obstacles
    • Magnet - Attracts collectibles
    • Slow Time - Slows down obstacles
    • Extra Life - Gives you an additional life
  • The game gets faster as your level increases
  • Try different game modes for varied difficulty
