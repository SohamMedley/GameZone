Snake Game

Control a hungry snake and eat as much food as possible

Score: 0
High Score: 0
Length: 3

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys or WASD to control the snake
  • Eat food to grow longer and increase your score
  • Avoid hitting walls and your own body
  • Try different game modes for varied challenges:
    • Classic - Standard snake game with walls
    • No Walls - Snake wraps around the edges
    • Obstacles - Avoid additional obstacles
    • Portal - Teleport through portals
  • Mobile users can use the on-screen controls
